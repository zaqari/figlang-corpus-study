Instructions

Figurative utterances (FIG) are not literally true in context. Some figurative language uses include:

Hyperbole:"This is the best day ever!"
*In a context in which it is a normal, or even just okay, day*

Understatement: "It's only a little bit gross"
*in a context where the speaker clearly thinks "it" is gross*

Sarcasm: "Smooth move"
*when the speaker doesn't think whatever happened was "smooth".*

Metaphor: "You're a little piggy"
*when the person being referred to is clearly not a pig*

Pretend Play:"I'm a little piggy! @oink @oink @oink"
*Someone pretending to be something or someone they are not.*
